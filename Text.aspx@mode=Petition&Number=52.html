Bermuda in Northeastern Jurisdiction (80052-CO-¶37-C)

Amend Section VII. Boundaries ¶ 37 Article I, line 3 following the word “Northeastern” to include the word “Bermuda.”

Rationale

The Baltimore-Washington Conference has been appointing pastors, superintending the congregations and integrating the congregations into the life of the Baltimore-Washington Conference; and the General Board of Global Ministries and the Baltimore-Washington Conference have recognized the churches as United Methodist congregations.
