Extended Cabinet (81295-IC-¶608.b)

Amend ¶608.b
  
It is recommended that t  The director shall be a part of the extended cabinet (or equivalent leadership structure) to provide advice and counsel concerning communications strategies relating to the conference program, conference budget  apportionment and benevolence interpretation, crisis management, and other matters as the cabinet and director may determine.

Rationale

By mandating this linking position at a place in the annual conference where many budgetary and programmatic decisions are made, communications ministry will be better represented at the table to improve the flow of information to and from the conference.
