Mandatory Training (80963-MH-¶335)

Amend ¶  335 as follows:
(5) completed an anti-racism training; (6) prepared and preached at least one written sermon on a biblical passage specified by the board of ordained ministry; (7) presented a plan and outline for teaching a book or books of the Bible; (8) responded to a written or oral doctrinal examination

Rationale

This petition acknowledges that racism and the reality of white privilege continues to be pervasive in the United States, including the church. This degrades the spiritual and moral level of the individual. Anti-racism training increases the effectiveness of elders to bring the good news of Christ to all.
