Leaves of Absence for Deacons (80076-MH-¶326.1)

Amend ¶326.1 as follows:  The commissioned ministers planning to give their lives as deacons in full connection shall be in ministries of Word and Service the entire probationary period .
  
Amend ¶330.1 as follows:  Served under Episcopal appointment in a ministry of service the entire probationary period.  for at least three full annual conference years.

Amend ¶355.1 as follows:
This relationship is granted to diaconal ministers, clergy who are local pastors, probationary members, associate members, and clergy members in full connection who, because of an immediate family member’s need for full-time care, are temporarily unwilling or unable to perform the duties of a full-time itinerant ministry  an appointed ministry.

Rationale

The proposed changes will resolve the problem of inconsistencies in regard to the Order of Deacons in the matter of leaves of absence.
