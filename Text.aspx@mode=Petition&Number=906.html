Purpose (80906-FO-¶128)

Amend ¶128:  
  
    The people of God, who are the church made visible in the world, must convince the world of the reality of the gospel or leave it unconvinced. There can be no evasion or delegation of this responsibility; the church is either faithful as a witnessing and serving community, or it loses its vitality and its impact on an unbelieving world.  By means of all its expressions, including business meetings, personnel matters, study classes, and planning sessions as well as worship services, the Church shall seek to perfect its love of God and neighbor.

Rationale

It’s by our fruits that we will be known.  “They’ll know we are Christian by our love.”  We need to say it out loud to remind ourselves that’s why we’re here, to be a blessing to the nations (Genesis 28:14).
