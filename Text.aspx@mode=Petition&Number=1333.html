Accessibility (81333-C2-¶162.G)

Amend ¶162.G as follows:
We recognize...  We urge the Church and society to recognize and receive the gifts of persons with disabilities to enable them to be full participants in the community of faith.  In that spirit, we request that the Church ensure that its facilities, both current and future, are fully accessible to persons with disabilities including but not limited to its building and room entrances and exits, chancel areas, bathrooms, and seating areas.  Furthermore, the Church will strive to make its worship, programs and services inclusive of and accessible to persons with disabilities, both physical and mental, as well as to those who provide care for them.  We call the Church...
