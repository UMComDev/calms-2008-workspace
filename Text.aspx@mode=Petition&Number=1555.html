Expulsion (81555-MH-¶1415.1)

Amend ¶1415.1 by adding a new subsection b) and lettering the existing paragraph as a) as follows:
  
¶  1415. Purposes and Objectives-1. a) To establish the criteria that must be met by schools, colleges, universities, and theological schools to achieve and retain listing as institutions affiliated with The United Methodist Church. b) No school shall be listed as an institution affiliated with The United Methodist Church unless its official policy as regards all alcoholic beverages is that no alcoholic beverage shall be possessed or consumed on any school property including affiliated property such as fraternities, sororities, or other leased or registered housing units with the violation of these rules being grounds for expulsion from the college or university.
