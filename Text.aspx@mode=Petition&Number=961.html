Open Membership (80961-LC-¶214)

Amend by addition to ¶¶ 214 and 225
¶ 214: ...may recite the appropriate vows on their behalf. Membership in the United Methodist Church shall not be denied by the pastor on the basis of race, gender, sexual orientation, ability, disability, national origin or economic status.
¶ 225: ...recommendation shall be listed as “Received From Other Denominations.”  Membership in the United Methodist Church shall not be denied by the pastor on the basis of race, gender, sexual orientation, ability, disability, national origin or economic status.

Rationale

The 2004 Book of Discipline says “God’s grace is available to all and Article IV acknowledges that all persons are of sacred worth.  We are called not to demonstrate bias or discrimination.  This petition puts us in alignment with the Discipline and the Bible.
