Limit Referral (80918-JA-¶362.1d3)

Amend ¶362.1d)(3) as follows:
  
   (3) refer the matter as an administrative complaint (P 362.2) or judicial complaint (P 2704).  The bishop shall not have the discretion of referring a complaint that charges any of the offenses in Paragraph 2702.1 as an administrative complaint.  

Rationale

In my experience, the administrative procedures are more subject to the influence of command by the bishop and require no clear level of proof than the opinions of Cabinet members.  A chargeable offense should be handled with greater care and require “clear and convincing” evidence (P 2711.2).
