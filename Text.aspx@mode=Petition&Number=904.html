Judicial Council Powers (80904-JA-¶2609)

Amend ¶2609 by adding a new subparagraph 9 as follows and renumbering the remaining text:
  
9. The Judicial Council shall not have the power to make decisions that relate to matters of biblical interpretation, Christology, Theology and ministry that have not been discussed and debated and acted upon in those settings that help form and shape understandings of the mission and ministry of the United Methodist Church.


Rationale

1. The Judicial Council is neither a legislative, executive nor biblical/theological body as it performs its important role of "...determ(ing) the constitutionality of any act of the General Conference..."
