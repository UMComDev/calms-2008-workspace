Health Requirements (81059-MH-¶324.8)

Amend ¶ 324.8 as follows:
    8. Each candidate   shall  present a satisfactory certificate of good health by a physician on the prescribed form.  provide evidence through appropriate wellness or health habit assessment tools, of self-care and stewardship practices regarding physical, emotional, relational, mental, and spiritual health.  Disabilities are not to be construed as unfavorable health factors when a person with a disability is capable of meeting the professional standards and is able to render effective service as a probationary member and commissioned minister.
