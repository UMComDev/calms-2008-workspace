Voice and Vote for Associate Members and Local Pastors on Board of Ordained Ministry (80009-MH-¶634.1)

Amend ¶634.1 as follows:
  
¶634.1 Each annual conference at the first session following the General conference shall elect for a term of four years a board of ordained ministry. At least six ordained elders, and deacons in full connection, and when possible, at least two associate members or local pastors who have completed the course of study shall be included as members of the board. All clergy members shall have voice and vote. Each annual conference shall elect at least one-fifth laypersons, which may include diaconal ministers, and may at its discretion elect further lay members, up to one third of the membership of the board...(The remainder of ¶634.1 is unchanged.)
