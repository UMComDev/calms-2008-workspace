ARC Responsibilities (80912-JA-¶635)

    Amend ¶635:  
    ...Its only  two main purposes shall be to ensure that the disciplinary procedures for involuntary leave of absence (P 354.1b), involuntary retirement, (P 359.3), or administrative location (P 362.4c)(3) are properly followed and that the grounds for this action were properly verified and support the action.  The pastor shall then have the right of appeal to the Jurisdictional Committee on Appeals (JCDs 799 and 830) within 30 days of the ruling of the administrative review committee, notifying the chairperson of the jurisdictional committee on appeals, the chairperson of the Administrative Review Committee, the conference secretary, and whoever else may be holding the documents and decisions being appealed .
    
        In addition, the amendment needs to be added to other paragraphs of the Book of Discipline, including Paragraphs 354.1b, 359.3, 362.4c(3).

Rationale

    In order to fulfill obligations the Judicial Council (Decisions 799 and 830) has given jurisdictional committees on appeal to rule on administrative actions under P 362 et al, the most logical group (Administrative Review Committee) must be empowered to cover both process and substance as the committee on appeals does.
