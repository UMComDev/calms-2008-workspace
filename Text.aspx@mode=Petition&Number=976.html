Definition (80976-DI-¶649.2)

Amend ¶649.2 as follows:
2. Membership- The membership of the council shall be young adults ( age nineteen to thirty  as defined by Annual Conference)

Rationale

The Book of Discipline currently limits the leadership of the Conference Council on young adults due to the parenthetical (age nineteen to thirty) Paragraph 649.2.
