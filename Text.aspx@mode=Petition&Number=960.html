Civil Rights (80960-C2-¶162.H)

Amend ¶162.H:
Equal Rights Regardless of Sexual Orientation  Certain basic human rights and civil liberties are due all persons.   We are committed to supporting those rights and liberties for homosexual persons.  We see a clear issue of simple justice in protecting their rightful claims where they have shared material resources, pensions, guardian relationships, mutual powers of attorney, civil unions and other such lawful claims typically attendant to contractual relationships that involve shared contributions, responsibilities, and liabilities, and equal protection before the law....  

Rationale

The United Methodist Church supports equal rights regardless of sexual orientation.  This addition brings us into alignment with the states that have already recognized civil unions.
