  ¶ 1212. Division Funding-1. The operating funds for the Division shall be derived from three main sources: World Service Fund, self-funding programs, and the general portion of the Youth Service Fund.
2. All real property and financial assets of the United Methodist Youth Organizatiion and all real property and financial assets of the Shared Mission Focus on Young People will be reassigned to the Division on Ministry With Young People.
This legislation will take effect upon the conclusion of the 2004 General Conference. The General Board of Discipleship, Shared Mission Focus on Young People, and United Methodist Youth Organization have the remainder of 2004 to make transitions into the new Division.
