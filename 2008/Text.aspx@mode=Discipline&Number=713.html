  ¶ 713. Election of General Secretaries of Program Agencies-The general secretary of each general program agency that is accountable to the Connectional Table shall be elected annually by ballot of the board of the agency involved. Each general program agency shall elect annually by ballot its deputy and associate general secretary(ies) and may elect or appoint such other staff as may be necessary.12
  
12. See Judicial Council Decisions 499, 567.
