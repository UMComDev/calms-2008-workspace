  ¶ 545. Records and Archives-1. The journal of the proceedings of
  a central conference, duly signed by the president and secretary, shall
be sent for examination to the General Conference through its
secretary.
2. The secretary of a central conference in which one or more bishops have been chosen shall report to the secretary of the General Conference the names of the bishop or bishops and the residences to which they have been assigned by the central conference.
