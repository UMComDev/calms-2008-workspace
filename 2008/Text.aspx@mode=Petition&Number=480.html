Media Resource Libraries (80480-LC-¶256)

Add new sub-paragraph to ¶256 Program Ministries as follows:
  
   Church Media Resource Library ministry.  To further the church’s educational nurturing ministry, a church library should be established wherever possible.

Rationale

The 1992 Discipline included these words under 264 Program Agencies 1.d.  They do not appear in subsequent Disciplines.  The church media resource library carries out the mission of the church by providing information through media resources that strengthen and support the total church ministry, and by providing biblically based resources...
