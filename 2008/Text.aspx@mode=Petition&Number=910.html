Committee on Appeals (80910-CO-¶500)

Amend by adding new ¶530 and renumber accordingly:  
  
    ¶530.  Committee on Appeals—1. There shall be a committee on appeals to deal with appeals of administrative and judicial actions in the annual conferences and any other tasks assigned by the General Conference.
    2. Its members shall be nominated from the floor of the Jurisdictional Conference with no more than one member elected from any annual conference.  Election shall be for eight years, except that half may be elected for four years to establish a rotation.  Two alternates shall be elected for four years at each quadrennial Jurisdictional Conference.  
    3. The duties of the committee are listed in Paragraphs 2715-2716.
    4. The committee shall meet at Jurisdictional Conference or within sixty days thereafter to elect officers and consider with a legal staff member from General Council on Finance and Administration and then establish what training they will need to function appropriately.
    5. Upon receipt of notice of appeal from a respondent, with copies to the presider of the trial/hearing whose decisions are being appealed, the committee shall meet within 60 days of that notification and shall return a ruling within ninety days, unless the respondent agrees to an extension.
   In addition, this legislation needs to correlate with Paragraphs 635 & 2713-2716.

Rationale

The Discipline does not provide for a committee on appeals in the section on the Jurisdictional Conference.  This petition requires more rapid establishment of its hearing and sets time limits for ruling.  It also includes the possibility that Administrative Review Committee rulings may be appealed.
