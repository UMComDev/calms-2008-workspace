Clergy Families in Crisis (81074-FA-NonDis)

The General Conference directs that each Annual Conference will appoint a task force to study and to recommend an official body, either new or existing within the Annual Conference, to be responsible for putting together and making available to families of clergy an explanation of the requirements for clergy; the rights of spouses during a marital separation or divorce and appropriate support and assistance in accordance with the Book of Discipline and the General Board of Pension and Health Benefits.
