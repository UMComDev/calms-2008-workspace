Retain Language (80041-FO-¶304)

  We respectfully petition the 2008 General Conference to retain the language concerning sexuality in ¶ 304.2 and .3 in the 2004 Book of Discipline.  

Rationale

  The United Methodist Social Principles declare, “We support laws in civil society that define marriage as the union of one man and one woman" (¶ 161C, 2004 Discipline).
