Implementing Resolution (80799-C2-NonDis)

  The 2008 General Conference of The United Methodist Church adopts “After the Fact: Reducing the Risk of Suicide Among Sexual Minority Youth,” as a resource as a good faith effort to begin to live toward the commitment made by the General Conference in 2004, as found in “Teen Sexual Identity and Suicide Risk,” resolution #158 of the Book of Resolutions of The United Methodist Church – 2004 (p. 410).

Rationale

The 2004 General Conference voted to create a task force to study the connection between teen sexual identity and suicide risk. Sadly, this study was not funded. While the church remains largely silent, precious lives continue to be lost. After the Fact is a step toward compassion and healing.
