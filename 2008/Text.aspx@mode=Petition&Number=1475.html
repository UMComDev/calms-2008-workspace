Jury Pool (81475-JA-¶2713.3a)

Amend ¶2713.3a as follows:
  
All appointments to the pool shall be made by the district superintendents  Conference Board of Ordained Ministry using a random selection process from all elders in the conference.

Rationale

Let us face the truth. In the real world in which we live, do you think the superintendents are going to appoint people that they feel would not favor the Church? If these $88,000 per year employees are allowed to choose the jury pool, that jury pool is already tainted...
