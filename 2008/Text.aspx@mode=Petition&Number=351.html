Leadership (80351-SU-¶414)

Add new sub-paragraph after ¶ 414.10
11.  To promote, support, and model generous Christian giving, with special attention to teaching the Biblical principles of giving.

Rationale

It is important that all levels of the superintendency actively make giving a part of their personal ministry and teaching in support of Christian discipleship in the clergy and lay members in their episcopal areas.
