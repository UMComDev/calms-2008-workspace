Annual Conference Investments (80544-FA-¶1508.7)

Amend ¶1508.7 a) as follows:
  
There shall be printed in the annual conference journal a list of the investments held by each agency handling such funds directly or indirectly under the control of the annual conference, or such list may be distributed directly to the members of the annual conference at their request.   A copy of all such lists of investments shall be filed annually with the General Board of Pension and Health Benefits.

Rationale

The current paragraph does not conform to current practice.
