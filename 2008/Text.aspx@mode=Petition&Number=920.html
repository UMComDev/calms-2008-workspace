Rationale

¶362.1 is not a substitute for "Supervision" as defined in ¶421.  It is a "complaint" process.  If Superintendents supervise properly, most complaints are prevented.  There should be no confusion when "supervision" (¶421) occurs and when a complaint process (¶362) begins.
