Chastity (80905-FO-¶304.2)

Amend  ¶304.2 in the following manner:  
  
   ". . . . To this end they agree to exercise responsible self-control by personal habits conducive to bodily health, mental and emotional maturity, integrity in all personal relationships, fidelity in marriage and celibacy  chastity in singleness . . . ."  
   In addition, the correction needs to be made everywhere the word “celibacy” is misused in the Book of Discipline, including ¶¶ 311.3f), footnote 3. to 311.3f), 322.1(8), 324.9(o), 330.4a)(6), 335.a(6), and 2702.1.

Rationale

"Celibacy" means simply to be "unmarried."  The traditional meaning does not relate to whether the celibate person has conjugal relations. Our relationships with those churches which know what these words mean are harmed by leaving the incorrect term in place.
