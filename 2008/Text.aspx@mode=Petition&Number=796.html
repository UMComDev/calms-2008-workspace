Online Access to The Book of Discipline (80796-IC-NonDis)

  The 2008 General Conference directs that The Book of Discipline of the United Methodist Church be made available to church members and the general public on a Church authorized/administered website for a subscribers’ fee. This is not intended to preclude continuing to publish The Book of Discipline is its current form.

Rationale

  The Book of Discipline available to church members and the general public on a church authorized/administered website would significantly reduce publication costs per copy, while making the Discipline more available to church members and the general public with no loss of revenue for the United Methodist Church.
