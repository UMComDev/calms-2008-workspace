Scriptural Authority (80453-FO-¶104)

Amend the first sentence of the second paragraph under Scripture in ¶104 of the 2004 Book of Discipline as follows:
  
     The biblical authors, though also subject to human and cultural limitations, were illumined by the Holy Spirit, and bear witness that in Christ the world is reconciled to God.
