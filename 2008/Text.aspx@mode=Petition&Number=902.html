Intimidation (80902-C2-¶162.A)

Amend ¶162.A as follows:
  
  A) Rights of Racial and Ethnic Persons-Racism is the combination of the power to dominate by one race over other races and a value system that assumes that the dominant race is innately superior to the others... We support affirmative action as one method of addressing the inequalities and discriminatory practices within our Church and society.
We condemn the use of nooses to intimidate persons for whom the noose is a stark reminder of the history of lynchings as a form of "vigilante justice" in the USA. We urge legislation in those states where legislation does not exist, to prosecute those who display nooses to harass and intimidate racial and ethnic persons.
