Health Requirements (81062-MH-¶315.5a2c)

Amend ¶ 315.5a.(2)c) License for Pastoral Ministry
    2) c) Provided the board with a satisfactory certificate of good health on a prescribed form from a physician approved by that board.  Shall provide evidence through appropriate wellness or health habit assessment tools, of self-care and stewardship practices regarding physical, emotional, relational, mental, and spiritual health.
