Maximum Service (81272-LC-¶259.2b)

Amend ¶259.2.b as follows:

b) In order to secure ... on the first year one class shall be elected for one year, one class for two years, and one class for three years. No person shall serve for more than six years in any consecutive nine years. Retiring members of the committee shall not succeed themselves. When vacancies occur...

Rationale

Most PPRC/SPRC members need 1-2 years to understand the committee’s work. Just as they are becoming productive, they must leave because they are at the end of their 3-year term. The would allow them to serve 6 years, enough time to provide several years of seasoned leadership.
