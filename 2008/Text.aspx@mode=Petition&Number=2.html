Recognition of Katanga Methodist University (80002-FA-¶811)

The 2008 General Conference of The United Methodist Church recognizes Katanga Methodist University as an approved United Methodist university benefiting from the privileges thereof by adding the following to ¶811:
  
8. No general council, board, commission, committee, or institution receiving general Church funds shall interpret one form of approved support (apportioned general funds, World Service Special Gifts, Advance Special Gifts, special appeals) as constituting greater or lesser approval by General Conference of the supported entity itself than of similar entities supported by other approved forms of support.
