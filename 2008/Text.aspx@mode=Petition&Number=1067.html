General Agency Support (81067-FA-¶806.1b)

Add new §4 and renumber remaining text:
806.1.b.4   The Connectional Table in consultation with the General Council on Finance and Administration, will determine an appropriate percentage of all funds received from conferences in fulfillment of apportionment obligations for particular agencies under the World Service Fund budget for the purpose of underwriting those costs necessary for the coordination and administration of those ministries.

Rationale

This supports legislation designed to give conferences the option of apportioning either the World Service Fund or by each agency under World Service.  Should conferences choose to apportion by agency, this gives GCFA and the Connectional Table explicit authorization to use an agreed upon percentage of that money for GCFA...
