Episcopal Area Representation (81293-CO-¶586.3)

Amend ¶586.3 as follows:
¶586.3  Missionary conferences shall elect clergy and lay delegates to General and jurisdictional conferences on the same basis as annual conferences as provided in  ¶¶502 and 513  ¶502.3.d.

Rationale

This petition would make parallel changes to this paragraph necessitated by other petitions, to provide each episcopal area with at least two delegates. The changes would result in fairer representation.
