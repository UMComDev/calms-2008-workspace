Online Course of Study (80862-MH-¶1421.3d)

Amend ¶¶1421.3d, 322.1.3, and 324.6c as follows:  
¶1421.3.d: Prescribe a Course of Study for local pastors that include studies for license for pastoral ministry and the five-year Course of Study curriculum. All work in the Course of Study shall be taken in programs approved by the Division of Ordained Ministry. Upon approval of the Conference Board of Ordained Ministry, a candidate may complete up to one-half of the work through on-line courses developed by the Division of Ordained Ministry.  

¶322.1(3) completed the five-year Course of Study for ordained ministry in addition to the studies for license as a local pastor, no more than one year  up to one half of which may be taken by correspondence or online/distance learning courses

¶324.6.c) completed the five-year Course of Study for ordained ministry, of which no more than four courses  one-half may be taken by correspondence or Internet; up to one half of Course of Study may be on-line courses;
