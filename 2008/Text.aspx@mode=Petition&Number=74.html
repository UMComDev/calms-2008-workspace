Affiliated Institutions (80074-MH-¶1415.3)

Amend ¶1415.3 as follows:
To provide an effective review process to ensure that schools, colleges, universities, and theological schools listed by the University Senate and qualifying for Church support have institutional integrity, well-structured programs, sound management, and clearly defined Church relationships and a mission and philosophy consistent with the mission and doctrine of The United Methodist Church.

Rationale

As institutions affiliated with The United Methodist Church, these schools are part of the Church’s mission and ministry, receiving name, credibility, and financial support from the Church.  As such, they should be expected to carry out their work in accordance with the mission and doctrine of The United Methodist Church.
