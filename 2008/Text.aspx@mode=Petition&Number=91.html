Pastor-in-charge (80091-MH-¶340)

  Amend ¶340 as follows:
¶  340. Responsibilities and Duties of Elders and Licensed Pastors - The responsibilities of elders and licensed pastors are derived from the authority given in ordination. Elders have a four-fold ministry of Word, Sacrament, Order and Service within the connection and thus serve in the church and the world. Local pastors share with the elders the responsibilities and duties of a pastor  pastor-in-charge for this four-fold ministry.
22. See Judicial Council Decision 579.
1. Word and ecclesial acts:
    a) To preach the Word of God, supervise the worship life of the congregation and lead in worship, read and teach the Scriptures, and engage the people in study and witness.24

Rationale

“Pastor in charge” is the modern way of stating the classic Methodist title of preacher-in-charge. This designation is basic in the United Methodist ecclesiology of pastoral stewardship.  Prior to 2004, the phrase about worship was ‘oversee the worship life of the congregation.’  This petition returns to that concept.
