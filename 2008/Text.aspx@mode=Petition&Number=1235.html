Commission Membership (81235-FA-¶624.1)

Amend ¶ 624 as follows:
  
¶ 624. Equitable Compensation  1. There shall be in each annual conference a commission on equitable compensation or other structure to provide for these functions and maintain the connectional relationships. It shall  is recommended that it be composed of an equal number...

Rationale

While recommending the allotment of membership of the Commission, it give the annual conference freedom to set the membership according to its needs.
