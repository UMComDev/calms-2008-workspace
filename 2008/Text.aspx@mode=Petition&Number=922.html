Specifications (80922-JA-¶362.1a)

Amend ¶362.1a:  
  
    A complaint is a written and signed statement claiming misconduct or unsatisfactory performance of ministerial duties which must include date, place, and specifics of events alleged to have occurred.

Rationale

    Most complaints tend to be opinions or perceptions.  Cabinet members should not be placed in the position of operating on someone else's opinion, but on actual facts that are the basis of the complainer's opinion.  Someone else may not agree with the opinion once the facts are known!
