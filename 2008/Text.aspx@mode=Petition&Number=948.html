Trial Pool (80948-JA-¶2712.3)

Amend ¶2712.3 as follows:
  
    3. The trial shall be convened as provided in ¶ 2709 with the pool of thirty-five or more persons to consist of clergy in full connection named by the College of Bishops  chosen by lot, the names drawn randomly in the presence of the counsels for the church and the respondent to assure that all appointees fit the disciplinary requirements, in approximately equal numbers from each episcopal area within the jurisdictional or central conference. Special consideration should be given so that the pool includes persons representative of racial, ethnic, and gender diversity.  
   [In addition, the correction needs to be made in other paragraphs of the Book of Discipline, including ¶¶ 2713.3a), and 2714.3]

Rationale

Under the current Discipline, the complaint going to trial has been considered by one or more members of the College of Bishops and they should not be in a position to influence the choice of members of the trial court.
