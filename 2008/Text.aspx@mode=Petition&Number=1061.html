Stewardship of Health (81061-MH-¶334.2)

Add to ¶ 334.2  Ministry, Authority, and Responsibilities of an Elder in Full Connection
  
    f) Shall annually provide evidence through appropriate wellness or health habit assessment tools, of self-care and stewardship practices regarding physical, emotional, relational, mental, and spiritual health to appropriate supervisory persons and/or bodies.  

Rationale

Our clergy are the human resources/assets of ministry.  This would assist our clergy to be accountable regarding intentionality of practicing quality self-care concerning mental, emotional, relational, physical, and spiritual health. The target result would be to improve both the quality of life and effectiveness of our clergy.
