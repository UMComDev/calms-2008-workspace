Social Principles (80850-C1-¶158)

Add a sentence at the end of the last paragraph of the Preface to the Social Principles:  
The Social Principles are printed in the Book of Resolutions.

Rationale

This petition is tied to several other petitions which seek to remove ¶ 160—165 from the Book of Discipline, since they are already printed in the Book of Resolutions.
