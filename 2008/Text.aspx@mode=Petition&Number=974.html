Investment Management (80974-FA-NonDis)

The 2008 General Conference directs all United Methodist Church-affiliated investors to persuade their respective investment managers to develop and maintain ethical investment policies that consider environmental, social and governance criteria consistent with the Social Principles and the United Nations Principles for Responsible Investing.
The 2008 General Conference also directs the General Board of Pension and Health Benefits and the General Board of Church and Society to develop and distribute guidelines that articulate ethical investment standards that reflect the values of The United Methodist Church for investment management firms engaged by all United Methodist Church-affiliated organizations.
