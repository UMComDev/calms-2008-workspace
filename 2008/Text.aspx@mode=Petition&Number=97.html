Amend Paragraph No. 259.1d as follows:
  
            “In order to secure...Retiring members of the committee shall not succeed themselves.  Only one person from an immediate family residing in the same household shall serve on the committee.  When vacancies occur...

Rationale

When several generations of one family are in a congregation they can successfully control the church by nominating two or more family members to this committee.  Presently, this is possible if family members live in different households.  If the intent is to insure diversity, the wording should be amended.
