Retired Health Plan (81214-FA-NonDis)

The General Board of Pension and Health Benefits of the United Methodist Church shall develop and present to the 2012 General Conference of the United Methodist Church a mandatory denomination group health plan for retired or disabled Medicare eligible United Methodist Clergy.  The General Board of Pension and Health Benefits of The United Methodist Church shall bill the Annual Conference(s) or the General Agencies in which service was recognized and rendered for the mandatory denominational group health Plan for retired or disabled Medicare eligible clergy in proportion to the years of pension and benefit responsibility found in the service records of the retired or disabled clergy member.
