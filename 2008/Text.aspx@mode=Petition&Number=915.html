Face Accuser (80915-JA-¶2701)

Amend by addition to ¶2701 and renumber accordingly:  
  
   5. The respondent has the right to face the one whose complaint originally led to all hearings under fair process.

Rationale

By not requiring the complainant to be present for any hearings, our denomination is telling the world we are incapable of protecting alleging victims whose complaints precipitated our processes.  In civil court, the failure of the complainant to appear leads to dropping the complaint.
