Voting Prior to Ordination (80975-MH-¶35-C)

Amend ¶35 as follows:
The ordained ministerial delegates to the General Conference and to the Jurisdictional or Central Conference shall be elected by and from the ordained ministerial members in full connection with the annual conference or provisional annual conference.

Rationale

Makes it possible for newly elected members to Annual Conference to vote prior to ordination.
