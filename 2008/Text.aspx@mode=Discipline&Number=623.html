  ¶ 623. Payment Obligation-Each church or charge has an obligation to pay the full compensation, as approved by the charge conference, to its pastor(s). If it becomes apparent that a church or charge will be unable to so provide the compensation approved by the charge conference, the church or charge shall immediately notify the district superintendent and may request consideration for a short-term emergency subsidy grant from the Equitable Compensation Fund (¶ 624.7).
