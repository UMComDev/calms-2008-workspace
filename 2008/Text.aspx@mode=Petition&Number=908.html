Ease Workload (80908-SU-¶424)

Amend by deletion ¶424 entitled “Program” and renumber.
  
    Program—1. To oversee the programs . . . .
  
  In addition, the program tasks listed should be referred to various annual conference boards, committees, and agencies and the appropriate Disciplinary passages should be corrected accordingly, with the recommendation that those Cabinet members with great talents in those various areas, should go off the Cabinet, take an appointment, and then serve on the conference or national church body related to that task.    

Rationale

Removing "Program" from responsibilities of Superintendents would give them more time to focus on supervision and on itineracy so they would match the pastors and churches better.  Currently, Superintendents use their time in sales for general church programs and "the long arm of the bishop" in conference program committees.
