Composite Petitions (81128-CO-¶507.2)

   Add one sentence to ¶ 507. 2:
    2. Each petition must...affects the others, the petition may call for the amendment of those paragraphs also to make them consistent with one another.  These composite petitions shall not be separated into pieces without the permission of the original maker.

Rationale

The maker of this petition is the original author of ¶ 507.2 which, in 1996, authorized composite petitions. In 2000, one of his composite petitions was broken apart with separate pieces going to different legislative committees defeating the purpose of ¶ 507.2.
