Discontinue Probationary Membership (81043-JA-¶362.5)

Delete ¶362.5 and add the same wording to the end of ¶362.4d, so that Paragraph 362.4d will read as follows:
d) Recommendation to Discontinue Probationary Membership---The board of ordained ministry shall recommend the discontinuance of a probationary member in keeping with the provisions of ¶327.6.   5. This process shall be reviewed by the Administrative Review Committee pursuant to ¶635.

Rationale

To clarify the original intent of the legislation that if a probationary member is discontinued because of an administrative complaint, the process must be reviewed by the Administrative Review Committee.
