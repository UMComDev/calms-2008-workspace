Youth Service Fund Delegate (81523-CO-¶602.4)

Amend ¶602.4 by addition after text:  
... president of the conference youth organization, chairperson of the Youth Service Fund, the chair of the annual conference college student organization...

Rationale

Many annual conferences require the Youth Service Fund chairperson to be present for a report or for fundraising purposes. As an important officer in the youth council, the YSF chair needs to have a voice and vote in the Annual Conference.
