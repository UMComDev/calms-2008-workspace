Judicial Council Powers (80849-JA-¶56.6-C)

The Constitution, Division Four—The Judiciary, Article II ¶ 56.6 be amended as follows:  
“To provide its own methods of organization and procedure where not otherwise specified in The United Methodist Book of Discipline”

Rationale

While the Constitution of the United Methodist Church clearly outlines the authority of the Judicial Council “To provide its own methods of organization and procedure” clarity is needed to ensure that the Council functions properly and does not overstep the broad definition provided by the Constitution.
