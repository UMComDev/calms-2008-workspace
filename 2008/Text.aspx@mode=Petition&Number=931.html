Personnel Files (80931-MH-¶606.9)

Amend ¶606.9 by addition:  
  
   d) individuals in whose name a record is kept shall have the right to add to any document in the file their response to the content of such a document, the response to be attached to that document.

Rationale

In normal work situations, people not only have access to their files but are to see any additions and have the right to add on a response which may provide information which puts that document into perspective.
