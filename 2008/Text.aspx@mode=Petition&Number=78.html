Clergy Evaluation (80078-MH-¶350)

Insert a new ¶350.3 as follows:
  
¶350.3. If three successive evaluations have indicated that the pastor is ineffective, and if in the judgment of the district superintendent the pastor is misplaced in ministry and not likely to become effective through additional counseling and training, then the superintendent will initiate the process for changing the conference relationship of the pastor under the complaint process as prescribed in ¶362.

Rationale

The current process for the evaluation of clergy performance, as measured by its success in resulting in a change in conference relationship of ineffective clergy, is not working.  It needs to be strengthened.
