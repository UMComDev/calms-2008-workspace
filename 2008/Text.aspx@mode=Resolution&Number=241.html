CHURCH AND STATE
241. Church Is a Weapon-Free Zone
  Whereas, in keeping with the spirit of Isaiah 2:4: God shall judge between the nations, and shall arbitrate for many peoples; they shall beat their swords into plowshares, and their spears into pruning hooks; nation shall not lift up sword against nation, neither shall they learn war any more;
Whereas, reflecting the church’s traditional role as a place of safety and sanctuary,
Therefore, every United Methodist church is officially declared a weapon-free zone.
