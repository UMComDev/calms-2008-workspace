Purpose (80911-CO-¶601)

Amend ¶601:  
  
    The purpose of the annual conference is to make disciples for Jesus Christ by equipping its local churches for ministry and by providing a connection for ministry beyond the local church; all to the glory of God.  In all its actions and activities, the annual conference shall seek to go on to perfection in love, as our heavenly Father is perfect.

Rationale

Our clergy promise at our ordination to go on to perfection and expect to achieve it in this life.  We must constantly seek that perfection in love as a conference and not just as individuals.  Our corporate actions will also speak louder than our words.
