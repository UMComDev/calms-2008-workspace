DCOM and Candidates (80337-MH-¶663.6)

Amend ¶663.6 as follows:
  
¶ 663.6—District Committee on Ordained Ministry
The vote of the committee on all matters of candidacy shall be by individual written ballot of the committee present. with  a  A three-fourths majority vote of the committee present  is required for certification. or approval or recommendation All other matters of candidacy shall be by a simple majority vote.

Rationale

To clarify and make consistent the language regarding the district committee on ordained ministry vote for candidacy certification and continuance of local pastors.
