Stem Cell Research (80843-C2-R9999)

Add a new resolution as follows:
  
WHEREAS, God calls each of us to full personhood and wishes for us well-being; and
  
WHEREAS, God desires health and healing for every human being; and
  
WHEREAS, some believe this century will be the "Century of Biology;"
  
WHEREAS, medical science will continue to discover new forms of healing for humankind; and
  
WHEREAS, Stem Cell Research, including somatic nuclear cell transfer, offers great potential for healing;
  
THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED that the 2008 General Conference join with other faith communities to support and dialogue with the medical and scientific communities concerning the ethical standards for its use; and
  
THEREFORE BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the General Board of Church and Society produce educational resources.  We encourage each pastor to use these resources to become informed concerning the debate regarding the use of Embryonic Stem Cells for medical research and to offer these resources for study in her or his local church.
