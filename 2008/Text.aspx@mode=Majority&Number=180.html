Delete the entire bolded portion of the petition and replace with the following:
A suitable permanent seat for the Judicial Council shall be selected by GCFA in consultation with the Judicial Counsel, with said location to provide sufficient space to permit the work of the Judicial Council and maintenance of records. The Judicial Council shall employ a clerk of the court as needed, but for an average of no more than 20 hours per week to assist in all matters of the court and funds for such operations shall be appropriated by the General Conference.
  
Budgetary Request: $25,000/year more than currently funded.
