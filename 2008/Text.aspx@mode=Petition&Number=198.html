National Comprehensive Plan for Town and Country Ministry (80198-GM-NonDis-$)

    We request that funding continue to be provided for implementation of the Action Plan for the National Comprehensive Plan for Town and Country Ministry through the budget of the General Board of Global Ministries at no less than $650,000 for the 2009-2012 quadrennium.

Rationale

The General Conference of 2000 approved Born Again in Every Place: the National Comprehensive Plan for Town and Country Ministry in the United Methodist Church, which affirmed the importance of town and country ministries and established general goals for the development and redevelopment of ministry in town and country settings...
