Witness (80921-JA-¶362.1a)

Amend ¶362.1a:  
  
    A complaint is a  written and signed statement written and signed by the alleging victim or an eye witness claiming misconduct or unsatisfactory performance of ministerial duties.

Rationale

    Current wording allows a superintendent to sign a complaint on behalf of the alleging victim, making the superintendent’s complaint hearsay.  Having the authority of the superintendent on the complaint when he or she was not a witness to the alleged events is an abuse of power.
