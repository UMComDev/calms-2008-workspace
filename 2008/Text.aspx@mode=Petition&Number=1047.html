Staff (81047-GA-¶714.1)

    Amend ¶714  
    Provisions Pertaining to Staff—1. No elected general program agency staff shall hold the same position more than twelve years.   Years of service prior to January 1, 1989 are not counted. The agency responsible for the election of such staff may annually suspend this provision by a two-thirds ballot vote.
