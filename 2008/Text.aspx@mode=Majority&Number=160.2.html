The Petition is adopted as amended as follows:

2.  Using the approved formula, GCFA will first calculate the total amount to be apportioned to each annual conference for all of the apportioned general funds.  Using formula (1), GCFA will first calculate the total amount to be apportioned to each annual conference for the following funds.  World Service, Ministerial Education Fund, General Administration Fund,  Africa University Fund, Interdenominational Cooperation Fund, and Black College Fund.

The Episcopal Fund shall be apportioned on the basis of Episcopal Fund allocations within each Jurisdiction among the Jurisdictional Conferences.  The amount to be apportioned to each Annual Conference is based upon the following formula:  (2)  A = K x F.  Where A represents an annual conference’s apportioned amount for the Episcopal Fund, K represents the annual conference’s share of the jurisdictions total apportioned World Service.  F represents the proportion on the Episcopal Fund assigned to the jurisdiction which equals the total Episcopal Fund allocations for that jurisdiction divided by total Jurisdictional Episcopal Fund allocations, times the total Episcopal Fund.
