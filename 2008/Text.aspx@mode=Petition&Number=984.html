Include Connectional Table (80984-FA-¶818.3)

Amend ¶ 818.3 as follows:
In the interim between the quadrennial sessions of the General Conference, such proposed churchwide financial appeals  shall require the approval of the General Council on Finance and Administration, the Connectional Table and the Council of Bishops. In case of emergency, the executive committee of either of these bodies may act in such manner for the body itself, but only by a three-fourths vote.

Rationale

Adding the Connectional Table in ¶ 818.3 ensures consistency with ¶ 818.2 enabling the Connectional Table to fulfill its function (¶ 901) as the church’s entity “where ministry and money are brought to the same table to coordinate the mission, ministries, and resources of the United Methodist Church.”
