Time of Testimony at General Conference (80099-CO-NonDis)

I petition the 2008 General Conference of the United Methodist Church to request the 2012 General Conference to set aside a period of time for the giving of testimony by gay, lesbian, bisexual, and transgendered delegates and alternates to General Conference regarding their faith and the work of God in their lives and ministries, and testimony by other delegates and alternates as to the faith and ministries of gay, lesbian, bisexual, and transgendered persons which they have witnessed (permission having first been obtained from the subjects of such testimony).  This opportunity will be scheduled at a time prior to debate or voting on any resolutions or changes to the Discipline which might be before the General Conference relating to homosexuality or the role of homosexual persons in the Church.  This will be a time of prayerful testimony, and any arguments regarding any resolutions or changes to the Discipline relating to homosexuality will not be made during this time but when such matters come before the General Conference for debate and vote.  Notice of this invitation and call for testimony will be included in materials distributed in preparation for General Conference and publicized in other appropriate ways.
