Collective Bargaining (81012-C1-¶163.B)

Amend ¶163.B as follows:
¶163 B) Collective Bargaining – We support the right of all public and private (including farm, government, institutional, and domestic) employees and employers to organize for collective bargaining into unions and other groups of their own choosing. ...

Rationale

  -    This new alternative is better language.
    -    A parenthesis can be exclusive in spite of its inclusive intention.
