Guidance of Our Bishops (80844-SU-NonDis)

The 2008 General Conference of the United Methodist Church respectfully requests the Council of Bishops to revisit “The Bishop’s Call For Peace And the Development Of Peoples, 1984” and “In Defense Of Creation, 1986” to prepare a pastoral message and study guide to address the United Methodist Church in light of new realities in which we live.

Rationale

“The Bishop’s Call For Peace And the Development Of Peoples, 1984” and “In Defense Of Creation, 1986” have served the people of the UM Church in shepherding our thinking around the difficult issues related to armed conflict.  In the intervening years automated battlefields and preemptive warfare have presented themselves.
