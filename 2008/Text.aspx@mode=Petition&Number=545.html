Contributions and Service (80545-FA-¶1506.22)

Amend ¶1506.22 as follows:
  
Optional provisions contained in the Ministerial Pension Plan, amended and restated effective January 1, 2007, as the Clergy Retirement Security Program, and Comprehensive Protection Plan may be adopted by vote of the annual conference subsequent to the receipt of a recommendation from the conference board of pensions. Effective January 1, 2007, contributions and credited service are determined in accordance with the provisions of the Clergy Retirement Security Program.

Delete the remainder of the paragraph.

Rationale

Aligns this paragraph with provisions of the Clergy Retirement Security Program which was approved by the 2004 General Conference and went into effect January 1, 2007.
