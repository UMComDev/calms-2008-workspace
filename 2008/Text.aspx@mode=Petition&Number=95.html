Expansion of UM Theme (80095-IC-NonDis)

I hereby petition the General Conference of the United Methodist Church to consider expanding and broadening our denominational theme to be, “Open hearts, Open minds, Open doors and Open Bibles.”

Rationale

Our theme, ”Open hearts, Open minds, Open doors” does not go far enough to convey our faith in the Holy Bible. This is an insufficient expression of who we say we are and, more importantly, who we should be.
