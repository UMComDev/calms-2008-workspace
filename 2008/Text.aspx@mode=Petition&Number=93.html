Inclusive Language for Age (80093-GA-¶4-C)

¶ 4. Article IV. Inclusiveness of the Church-The United Methodist Church is a part of the church universal, which is one Body in Christ. The United Methodist Church acknowledges that all persons are of sacred worth. All persons without regard to race, color, national origin, status,4  or economic condition, or age shall be eligible to attend its worship services, participate in its programs, receive the...
   ¶  161. II. THE NURTURING COMMUNITY
The community provides... Language of a derogatory nature (with regard to race, nationality, ethnic background, gender, sexuality, and physical differences, and age) does not reflect value for one another...
