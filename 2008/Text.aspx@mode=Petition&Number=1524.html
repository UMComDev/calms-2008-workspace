Percentage on Agencies and Boards (81524-GA-¶705.3h)

Amend ¶ 705.3h to read:
h) It is recommended  required that the membership of general agencies and boards include both youth (¶ 256.3) and young adults. It is recommended  required that, whenever possible, at least 10 20 percent of the members of each general agency and board be equally divided between youth and young adults. The youth and young adult membership of each general agency and board shall be inclusive (consistent with 705.3i).

Rationale

To ensure that general agencies and boards are inclusive of youth and young-adult voices and the general church is responsive to youth and young-adult needs.
