Climate Changes (80996-C1-¶160)

Add new paragraph called Climate Changes to the Social Principles ¶ 160 The Natural World:
  
We confess as sin the wounds we have given our planet. We are worried about the changes in temperatures, water level, weather and other environmental changes our lifestyle has caused in the nature, and suffer with all those whose homes are threatened. We emphasize the responsibility we have as a global society to carry out our stewardship for an ecologically equitable and sustainable world and by every effort possible let the nature’s wounds heal.  
We call on all countries in the world to commit to international agreements, and make radical decisions for the sake of the creation.

Rationale

Climate changes are already happening, and are threatening our world and a lot of people’s homes and livelihood. This cannot continue, and a part of our common and much needed fight for the nature is to make climate changes an outspoken issue in the social principles.
