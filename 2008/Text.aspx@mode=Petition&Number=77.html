Longer Tenure Criteria (80077-SU-¶434)

Amend ¶434, The Book of Discipline, 2004 by adding the following after the current final sentence:
Reassignment of pastors will not be considered if the following criteria are met:
1) The charge being considered is in a continual growth pattern both numerically and spiritually;
2) After full review of the status of the charge (including input from the annual consultations with the pastor and the Staff/Pastor-Parish Relations Committee), the district superintendent recommends the pastor’s continued appointment is necessary for the continued growth of the charge, and that recommendation is affirmed by the Cabinet and the Bishop; and
3) The charge is faithfully living out its mission as a part of the connectional United Methodist Church including full payment of conference claims and apportionments.

Rationale

The Discipline makes provision for long-term tenure to ensure a more effective ministry considering the needs of the charge, the community, and the pastor. This petition provides criteria to guide the determination of long-term pastorates providing for input from both clergy and laity.
