Written Financial Policies (80096-FA-¶259.4)

Amend ¶259.4 by adding a new subparagraph after b) as follows and relettering the current remaining:
  
c) The committee on finance shall establish written financial policies to document the internal controls of the local church.  The written financial policies should be reviewed for adequacy and effectiveness annually by the committee on finance and submitted as a report to the charge conference annually.

Rationale

Safeguarding assets is an important part of stewardship.  Fraud is more likely to occur when someone has "incentive", weak internal controls or oversight provide an "opportunity", and the person can "rationalize" the fraudulent behavior.  We need to protect our local churches by limiting the opportunity to commit fraud.
