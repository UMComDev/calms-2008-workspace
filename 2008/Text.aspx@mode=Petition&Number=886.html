Endowment Fund (80886-MH-¶633.4d)

Add new subparagraph after  ¶633.4.d) (10)
      (11) To encourage the development of a permanent endowment fund for Wesley Foundations and campus ministries; and to set polices and investment standards, in consultation with the conference foundation, for permanent endowment funds for Wesley Foundations and campus ministries.

Rationale

Unpredictable financial support means that campus ministry’s long-term health depends on the establishment of viable development plans.  Endowment funds provide stability during unpredictable giving cycles.  A part of any development plan is the creation of a permanent endowment fund.  Policies for these funds should be set by the conference BHECM.
