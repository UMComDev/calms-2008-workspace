Tenure (80983-GA-¶905.7d)

Delete ¶905.7d.

Rationale

Each agency is accountable to the General Conference. Including ¶ 905.7d in Connectional Table responsibilities is unnecessary given ¶ 714.1: “No elected general program agency staff shall hold the same position more than twelve years.” Each agency retains the right to annually suspend this provision by a two-thirds vote.
