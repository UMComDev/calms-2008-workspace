Records (80374-CO-¶606.8)

Amend ¶ No. 606.8       
8.   All Official records of secretaries, statisticians, and treasurers shall be kept according to the forms prepared by the General Council on Finance and Administration so that all official statistical and financial items shall be handled alike in all annual conferences and that uniformity of reporting shall be established as a churchwide  worldwide church policy.

Rationale

The Committee on Official Forms and Records has referred this language to clarify and amplify the need for control of reporting formats for the official records of all annual conferences.
