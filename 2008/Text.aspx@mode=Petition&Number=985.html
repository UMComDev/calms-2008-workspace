Budget Process (80985-GA-¶905.7a)

Amend ¶ 905.7a as follows:
To collaborate with the General Council on Finance and Administration in the preparation of budgets for the apportioned funds (¶ 806) , to submit to each quadrennial session of the General Conference budgets of expense for each of the general funds of the church, and to make recommendation regarding all other funding considerations to come before the General Conference.

Rationale

Deleting (¶ 806) clarifies the specific budgets for which the Connectional Table, in collaboration with GCFA, has responsibility. The current language lacks clarity because ¶ 806 contains a reference to ¶ 810.1, which identifies budgets beyond the official responsibility of the CT.
