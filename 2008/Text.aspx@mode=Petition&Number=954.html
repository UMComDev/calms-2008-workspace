Theological Dialogue (80954-FO-NonDis-!)

The General Board of Church and Society and General Board of Discipleship shall jointly conduct a dialogue with theologians, scientists, clergy and others both within and beyond The United Methodist Church to examine current Christian theologies and assess their relevance to current knowledge and circumstances. Funding for such a dialogue shall be met through readjustment of budgets approved by the General Council on Finance and Administration and/or application of grants and gifts designated for this purpose. Each Annual Conference of The United Methodist Church is encouraged to engage in similar dialogues in its geographical area. A report with recommendations for appropriate theological additions and clarifications for our day shall be submitted to the 2012 General Conference for adoption.

Rationale

The decline of mainline denominations can be traced in part to the clash of modern world views with ancient and medieval world views.  This petition, if adopted, would set in motion dialogues that could bridge the gap between those world views to the benefit of the Church.
